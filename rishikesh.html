Welcome to Rishikesh

The Yoga Capital of the World

About

Rishikesh is a peaceful town on the banks of the Ganges, known for yoga, adventure, and spirituality.

Top Attractions

Triveni Ghat, Laxman Jhula, Ram Jhula, and the scenic riverfront are must-see spots.

Best Time to Visit

February to May and September to November offer pleasant weather for exploring.
